The Crown Effect

by Berkay, Jessica, Lisa, Ozgur and Varun

The recent epidemic coronavirus creates uncertainties in the global health community, and it is challenging to communicate potential risks to the general public and public health professionals. Our project aims to provide policy makers and health professionals with reliable information regarding the future spread of coronavirus and help them take proactive measures, create an interactive user interface and comprehensive visualizations to make information about coronavirus easily digestible for the general public, and predict potential health risks and business risks in the future and support data-driven decision making among professionals.
